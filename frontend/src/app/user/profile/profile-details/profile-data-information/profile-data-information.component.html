Profile data

On this page you can change your personal data and your account data which will be used during your online purchases in the future.

In order to have the most secure and inaccessible account possible, we recommend that you do not create the password with characters or words that include:

  • Words that exist in the dictionary of any language.
  • Back spelled words, common spelling mistakes or abbreviations.
  • Repeating characters, such as: 12345678, 222222, abcdefg, or letters of a line or string on the keyboard (eg, qwerty).
  • Personal data, your name, date of birth, passport number, ID or similar data.
